Product Details

The Everlong Nutri-Blend is a fully automatic mixer grinder designed to simplify your kitchen tasks. With pre-programmed menus for a variety of foods and high-speed operation, it ensures quick and efficient results. Featuring multiple safety features, this blender includes two unbreakable transparent jars, stainless steel blending and grinding blades, a seasoner, and a sipper cap. The sleek, powerful motor supports hands-free operation, making it easy to create consistently smooth and delicious dishes every time.
    Key Features:
  • Powerful 350W Motor: Efficiently blends, grinds, and purees fruits, vegetables, and other ingredients for smooth, nutrient-packed drinks.
  • Multiple Pre-Programmed Functions: Includes settings for smoothies, shakes, soups, and more, allowing for easy one-touch blending.
  • High-Quality Stainless Steel Blades: Sharp and durable, designed to break down ingredients quickly and evenly for the perfect blend.
  • Compact Design: Sleek and space-saving, making it ideal for small kitchens and easy storage.
  • Easy to Use: Simple push-to-blend functionality for fast results without complicated settings.
  • Detachable Jars: Comes with two unbreakable transparent jars that can also be used as drinking cups, perfect for on-the-go use.
  • Leak-Proof Lids: Ensures secure blending without spills, and includes sipper and sealing caps for convenient use.
  • Dishwasher Safe: The jars and lids are easy to clean, either by hand or in the dishwasher.
