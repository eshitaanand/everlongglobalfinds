Product Details

Ironing becomes effortless with the Everlong Dry Iron. Say goodbye to wrinkled clothes – this iron smooths them out quickly, leaving them looking crisp and fresh. Unlike traditional irons, it's designed to be lightweight, making it easy and comfortable to use. Even with a pile of clothes to iron, you won’t experience hand fatigue, thanks to its lightweight build.
    Key Features:
  • Powerful 1000W Performance: Provides quick heat-up and efficient ironing, saving you time and effort.
  • Non-Stick Coated Soleplate: Ensures smooth gliding over fabrics, preventing sticking and ensuring wrinkle-free results.
  • Adjustable Temperature Control: Customize the heat settings to suit different fabrics, from delicate to heavy materials.
  • Ergonomic Design: Lightweight and comfortable to hold, reducing hand strain during long ironing sessions.
  • Indicator Light: Lights up when the iron is heating up or has reached the desired temperature.
  • Overheat Protection: Automatically shuts off to prevent overheating, ensuring safety and energy efficiency.
  • Easy-to-Use: Simple dial controls for precise temperature adjustment.
